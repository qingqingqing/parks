What is www.parks_ing.com?

www.parks-ing.com is a website to digest all of the reviews and present it on one page.


Why did I have this idea?

It is due to my trip to YellowStone National Park in May of 2014. It is time consuming to read all of reviews online. For example, there are hundreds of reviews about every of 84 attractions in YellowStone Naitonal Park on TripAdvisor.com. So I want to build an app to present people's opinions of something with only one page.


How? The process of building this.

There are four steps to build this website. The main techniques and tools are listed as below:

  1. Scraping www.tripadvisor.com: BeautifulSoup
  2. Storing data into mysql: MySQLdb
  3. Building machine learning models:Naive Bayes, Tag Clouds
  4. Designing and deploying website: bootstrap, Flask, AWS .

Google.com and stackoverflow.com are my teachers. w3schools.com helped me a lot too, especially the front end knowledge.
